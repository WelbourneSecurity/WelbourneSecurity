Reporting chain

From post reading to national picture

  1. Post observers GZI bearing and elevation, BPI pressure, and FSM radiation readings were reported from the monitoring post.
  2. Group Control Bearings from two or more posts were plotted on a table map to fix the burst location.
  3. Sector Control Sector scientific teams used the Group reports to build the broader fallout and warning picture.

Source Notes

This prototype uses town-level plotting for Group Controls and a representative public sample of posts. It avoids presenting exact access guidance for private or unsafe sites.
